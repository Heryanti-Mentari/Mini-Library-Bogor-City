Smart City Bogor

Bogor City embraces the Smart City concept to enhance public services, improve governance efficiency, and promote sustainable development. Through technology integration, Bogor aims to create a connected, innovative, and eco-friendly urban ecosystem.

Source

Profile of Bogor City

Located just south of Jakarta, Bogor City is renowned for its lush greenery, rich culture, and historical heritage. As one of Indonesia’s leading educational and tourism hubs, Bogor serves as a vital link between the capital and West Java.

Its cool climate, diverse population, and growing economy make it an ideal city for implementing Smart City innovations. The city government is committed to advancing digital transformation and sustainability.

With the motto “Bogor Smart, Green, and Heritage”, the local administration aims to integrate modern technology while preserving its cultural identity and natural environment.

Smart City Implementation in Bogor

The Smart City program in Bogor focuses on six main pillars — Smart Governance, Smart Economy, Smart Environment, Smart People, Smart Mobility, and Smart Living. Each pillar represents a strategic initiative to enhance public services, foster innovation, and ensure inclusivity.

Digital public services such as online licensing, e-budgeting, and citizen complaint systems (like “Bogor Single Window” and “SIAGA 112”) are already operational. In addition, Bogor promotes eco-friendly initiatives such as Smart Waste Management, renewable energy projects, and digital monitoring of green spaces.

Collaboration between the local government, private sector, and academic institutions continues to shape Bogor as a leading example of a sustainable and people-oriented Smart City in Indonesia.
